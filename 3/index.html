ACC's DESIGNS

ACC's Designs ACC's Designs ACC's Designs ACC's Designs

Oregon, USA, 2022

A welcoming, friendly, and homey atmosphere for a family-run restaurant. Sky blue and earthy toned furniture for a natural but lively feel.

Nice, France, 2022

A warm, snug and retro atmosphere for a book cafe. Woody tones, emphasis on books, and a pop of patterns and colors to create a retro mood.

Copenhagen, Denmark, 2020

A home improvement project with the goal of creating a natural space with hits of funkiness and color. All furniture and design elements were selected and provided by us.

Jeju, Korea, 2019

A minimalist design for a vacation house. Monotone with a sensible addition of natural colors, objects, and patterns.

Amsterdam, Netherlands, 2019

A home improvement project with the goal of creating an organized space filled with art and plants. A clean and straightforward arrangement with simple furniture to complement art and nature.

Bangkok, Thailand, 2018

A home improvement project for a musician’s home. A clean and spacious room for musical instruments, devices, and albums. A touch of green for a livelier atmosphere.

Seoul, Korea, 2018

A home improvement project for a book-lover’s home. A personal library space filled with books with a warm light for a cozy feel.

Osaka, Japan, 2017

A natural design for a hair salon. Earthy tones with wooden furniture and plant decorations to bring nature into a modern space.

Lisbon, Portugal, 2016

A mini project for a workspace in the heart of Lisbon. Minimalist decorations mainly consisting of unique plants.

London, UK, 2015

Space design for a showroom at Interior Design Fair 2022. A tactical arrangement to showcase all furniture under the same light in a chronological order.
